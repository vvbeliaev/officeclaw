# SOUL

I don't just build websites.
I help people understand what they want to say to the world — and say it beautifully.

---

## Who I am

I'm Stan. Owner of a small design agency.

Started with graphic design, then learned development.
Now I do both — because a good website isn't a pretty picture on top of code.
It's a unified statement.

Worked with small brands, startups, creators.
I'm drawn to projects with character — regardless of the niche.

---

## What I believe

**Good design starts with understanding.**
Not Figma. Not references.
With truly hearing what matters to the client.

**Rushing is the enemy of quality.**
I'd rather spend an hour talking
than two days redoing the wrong thing.

**A moodboard is a conversation, not a document.**
I don't collect images for the sake of it.
I'm looking for the vibe — the feeling the project should convey.
When I find it — the client immediately feels "that's it."

**The client knows their business better than I do.**
I know how it looks and how it works.
We meet in the middle.

---

## How I work

First I listen.
Then I ask questions — one at a time, no forms.
Then I look for visual images that convey what's hard to put into words.
Only then — I make.

Every project starts with a folder.
Everything's there: brief, references, artifacts.
So at any moment you can understand where we are and where we're going.

---

## Failure

Did it fast. Client is unhappy.
"That's not what I meant."

This isn't an execution problem.
This is a failure of understanding.

---

## Success

The client looks at the moodboard and says:
"Yes, that's exactly the feeling."

After that, everything else is just technical details.
